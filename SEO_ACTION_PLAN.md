### Step 1: Set Up Google Search Console (30 minutes)
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Add your property: `https://virtue-digital-detox.in`
3. Verify ownership using one of these methods:
   - **HTML file upload** (easiest)
   - **HTML meta tag** (already added to your site - just replace `YOUR_VERIFICATION_CODE_HERE`)
   - **Google Analytics** (after setting up GA4)
   - **Domain name provider**
4. Once verified, submit your sitemap: `https://virtue-digital-detox.in/sitemap.xml`

### Step 2: Set Up Google Analytics 4 (20 minutes)
1. Go to https://analytics.google.com
2. Create a new GA4 property for your website
3. Get your Measurement ID (format: G-XXXXXXXXXX)
4. Replace `G-XXXXXXXXXX` in your index.html with your actual Measurement ID
5. Test that it's working using the GA4 DebugView

### Step 3: Update Social Media Links (15 minutes)
Replace these placeholders in `index.html`:
- `YOUR_FACEBOOK_PAGE_URL` ‚Üí Your actual Facebook page URL
- `YOUR_INSTAGRAM_PAGE_URL` ‚Üí Your actual Instagram page URL  
- `YOUR_LINKEDIN_PAGE_URL` ‚Üí Your actual LinkedIn page URL
- `YOUR_TWITTER_HANDLE` ‚Üí Your Twitter handle (if you have one)
- `YOUR_FACEBOOK_APP_ID` ‚Üí Get this from Facebook Developers (optional but recommended)
- `@YOUR_TWITTER_HANDLE` ‚Üí Your Twitter handle

### Step 4: Create Google Business Profile (45 minutes)
1. Go to https://business.google.com
2. Create a business profile for "Virtue Digital Detox"
3. Add your:
   - Business name
   - Category: "Software Company" or "Health & Wellness"
   - Location (if you have a physical office)
   - Phone number
   - Website URL
   - Business hours
   - Description (use keywords: digital detox, screen time, wellbeing)
4. Upload your logo and photos
5. Verify your business
6. Update the Local Business schema in index.html with your actual address and coordinates
