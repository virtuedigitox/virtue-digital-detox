How Virtue Works

Smart Blocking That Adapts to You

Block distracting apps by schedule (e.g., 10 PM–7 AM) or by daily usage limits (e.g., 30 minutes/day). Choose your strictness level—from gentle reminders to iron-clad commitments.

  • Schedule-based blocking for specific times
  • Usage limit blocking with daily caps
  • Customizable strictness levels
  • Location-based triggers (coming soon)

Put Money on the Line

Real commitment means real stakes. Commit money to your goals—if you stay within limits, you keep your money and earn bonus rewards. If you exceed limits, you lose the commitment. This psychological accountability makes all the difference.

  • Money-based commitment for serious accountability
  • Ad-based commitment for free users
  • Watch mindful ads to extend usage
  • Higher stakes = higher rewards

Earn Real Rewards

Success feels good—and with Virtue, it pays off. Earn Virtue Coins through scratch cards when you meet your goals. Redeem coins for discounts, cashback, and exclusive offers from our partner ecosystem.
