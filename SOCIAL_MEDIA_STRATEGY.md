## ğŸš« What NOT to Do

1. **Don't buy followers** - Fake engagement hurts your reach
2. **Don't over-promote** - Follow 80/20 rule (80% value, 20% promotion)
3. **Don't ignore comments** - Engagement is crucial
4. **Don't post inconsistently** - Set a schedule and stick to it
5. **Don't use irrelevant hashtags** - Quality over quantity
6. **Don't steal content** - Always credit sources
7. **Don't be too salesy** - Build relationships first
